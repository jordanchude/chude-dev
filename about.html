Technical expertise meets small business reality

What sets our studio apart is the technical and operational background behind the work. Our developers have built and maintained production systems that reached millions of users, supported ecosystems with millions of active participants, and delivered features that drove measurable improvements in adoption, efficiency, and growth.

This includes building systems that improved feature adoption by double-digit percentages, reduced operational workflows by 25%, and supported revenue programs generating over $1.2M. We bring the same care, structure, and reliability expected in larger technology organizations, while tailoring solutions to the realities and budgets of small businesses. Rather than treating websites as static marketing pages, we approach them as business systems—each site designed around customer journeys and real-world behavior, especially on mobile where most discovery and decision-making happens.

The core values that drive everything we do

Join our team

Reliability first

We prioritize performance, accessibility, clarity, and local search visibility from the start. Our developers have deep experience working across the full web stack—from React and TypeScript frontends to Node.js, Go, and Python backends, deployed on AWS. This includes engineering fault-tolerant media pipelines, automated processing workflows, and secure file-handling systems that could not afford downtime or data loss. This allows us to build websites that are not only visually polished, but also stable, secure, and easy to maintain.

Business-focused

We work especially closely with restaurants and service-based small businesses that depend on local visibility and repeat customers. That means thoughtful presentation of menus, services, hours, and locations; seamless integrations for reservations, ordering, or inquiries; and layouts optimized for customers making quick decisions on their phones.

Ongoing partnership

As a small studio, we intentionally limit the number of projects we take on. This allows our developers to work directly with clients, understand business constraints, and take full ownership of delivery—from planning and build through launch and ongoing support. We don't disappear after a site goes live. Instead, we offer continued support so websites can evolve alongside the businesses they represent.

Let's build a website that removes friction

For customers trying to find and choose your business, and for you as an owner who wants a website that simply works. By combining professional engineering standards with a practical understanding of small business needs, we build websites that are durable, trustworthy, and designed to grow with your business over time.
