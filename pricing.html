Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our packages, pricing, and what's included. If you don't see your question here, feel free to reach out—we're happy to help.

What's included in the support period?

Support includes bug fixes, security updates, and help with technical issues. We also provide guidance on making content updates using the content management system we set up. For larger changes or new features, we can discuss additional work at our standard rates.

Can I customize a package or add features?

Absolutely. Our packages are designed to cover common needs, but every business is different. We can customize any package to add or remove features based on your specific requirements. Use the custom quote form on this page to tell us about your project, and we'll provide a tailored proposal.

How long does a typical project take?

Timeline depends on the package and project complexity. A Starter package typically takes 4-6 weeks from kickoff to launch. Business packages usually take 6-10 weeks, and Complete packages can take 10-14 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline in your project proposal based on your specific needs.

What happens after the support period ends?

We offer continued support beyond the included period. You can purchase ongoing support packages, or we can work on an as-needed basis for updates, new features, or technical issues. We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients, so we're here when you need us.

Do you work with restaurants specifically?

Yes, we work especially closely with restaurants. We understand the unique needs of restaurant websites—menu presentation, online ordering, reservations, hours, locations, and local search visibility. Our Business and Complete packages include features specifically designed for restaurants, but we can customize any package to fit your needs.

What if I need something not in any package?

No problem. Use the custom quote form on this page to tell us about your project. We'll review your requirements and provide a tailored proposal with clear deliverables and pricing. We're flexible and can work with a variety of needs, budgets, and timelines.
