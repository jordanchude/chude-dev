Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with our studio, our process, and what to expect when building your website.

How big is your studio team?

We're a boutique studio with a small, experienced team. We intentionally limit the number of projects we take on so our developers can work directly with clients, understand business constraints, and take full ownership of delivery. This means you'll work directly with the people building your site—no account managers or middlemen.

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work especially closely with restaurants and service-based small businesses that depend on local visibility and repeat customers. However, we've built websites for a variety of small businesses—from retail shops to professional services. If you're a small business owner who needs a website that actively supports your goals, we'd love to talk.

How can I request a project quote?

You can use the contact form on this page, or check out our packages page for feature-based pricing. We also offer custom quotes for projects that don't fit our standard packages. Just tell us about your project, timeline, and budget, and we'll provide a clear proposal tailored to your needs.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch?

Yes. We don't disappear after a site goes live. We offer continued support so your website can evolve alongside your business. With clean architecture and simple content management, you can make many updates yourself, but we're here when you need help with larger changes, new features, or technical issues.

What makes your approach different from other agencies?

We treat websites as business systems, not just marketing pages. Our developers have experience building production systems at scale, which means we bring enterprise-level care and reliability to small business projects. We focus on performance, accessibility, and local search from the start, and we emphasize clean architecture so you can update content confidently without constant developer help.

Can I see examples of websites you've built?

We'd be happy to share examples of our work during our initial conversation. We work with a variety of small businesses and restaurants, and we can show you sites that demonstrate our approach to performance, mobile optimization, local search, and business-focused design. Contact us to learn more about our portfolio.
